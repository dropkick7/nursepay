About NursePay

L&D nurse since 2013. New York then California. I built this between shifts because real pay info didn't exist. Just vague Reddit threads and guesses. So I made this site to be useful to all nurses.

Casey, RN and Founder of NursePay

Why NursePay exists

Hospitals don't post real numbers. Job listings are vague. The only way to find out what you should be earning is to know the right people. Most nurses don't.

NursePay fixes that. Real pay from real nurses, shared anonymously, so everyone can make better decisions.

How it works

Fill out a short form. No name, no login, no account. Every submission gets reviewed before it goes live. Nothing is collected that could identify you.

What we believe

Pay isn't everything. But it's a big thing. Knowing your worth helps you negotiate, move smarter, and stop being underpaid. This only works because nurses share. Every submission matters.

Built by a nurse, for nurses

Independent. No investors, no advertisers. Built by a working nurse between shifts. Just a genuine attempt to make nursing pay more transparent.
