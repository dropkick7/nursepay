Why I Built NursePay

I started out as an L&D nurse in New York back in 2013. Now I'm in California (yay ratios!) working across women's health, clinic, postpartum, L&D, you name it. I built this between shifts because I couldn't find real nurse pay info when job hunting. Just vague threads and guesses.

So I made what I wish existed: a place where nurses can just share what they earn. No fluff. No logins. Hope it helps the next nurse who's looking.

— Casey, RN & Founder of NursePay
