Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What we collect

NursePay is built on anonymity. When you submit pay data, we collect only the data fields in the form — no name, no employer, no email, no IP address tied to your submission. Submissions are anonymous by design.

Newsletter signups

If you sign up for the Per Diem Roundup newsletter, we collect your email address solely to send you the newsletter. We do not sell or share your email with anyone.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand general traffic patterns — things like how many people visit, which pages are popular, and where traffic comes from. This data is aggregated and anonymous. No individual users are tracked.

Cookies

Google Analytics uses cookies. We don't use any other cookies or tracking technologies.

Third parties

We use Fillout.com to power our submission forms. Their privacy policy applies to data submitted through those forms. We do not sell data to any third parties.

Your data

Since submissions are anonymous, we have no way to identify or delete your specific submission. If you signed up for the newsletter and want to be removed, just unsubscribe from any email or contact me here.
